A note from Brian McGuire

Founder and Owner

M-Tac Trucking Compliance LLC was established on January 1st of 2019.

I found that many carriers are in need of a point of contact for assistance in becoming and remaining in compliance with 49 CFR (The USDOT regulations governing commercial vehicle compliance). I will ensure you and your drivers are in compliance with all Federal and State regulations.

I have the knowledge necessary to review roadside inspections and advise on incorrectly documented violations, which is essential when challenging inspections to improve your CSA Safety Rating. I perform post-crash inspections and properly document my findings for future purposes, such as insurance claims.

Proper training is essential for all aspects of transportation safety, including driver safety, hours of service requirements, and performance or maintenance issues. Using a contemporary and aggressive approach, I will work alongside you and your company to ensure a thorough understanding of industry standards and that you and your employees have the tools necessary for the safe and compliant operation of your business.
