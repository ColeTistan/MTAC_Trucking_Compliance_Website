About Us

M-Tac Trucking Compliance LLC was found on January 1, 2019.

I found that many carriers are in need of a point of contact for assistance in becoming and remaining in compliance with 49 CFR (The USDOT regulations governing commercial vehicle compliance).  I will ensure you and your drivers are in compliance with all Federal and State Regulations.

I have the knowledge necessary to review roadside inspections and advise on incorrectly documented violations which is essential when challenging inspections. I will make myself available to perform post- crash inspections and properly document my findings for future purposes, such as insurance claims.  

Proper training is essential for all aspects of Transportation Safety, including Driver Safety, Hours of Service, Performance or Maintenance issues.  I will work with you to ensure your clients understand what the industry standards are and to improve compliance and safety management of their business. I will take a contemporary and aggressive approach to ensuring you have a safe and compliant operation.


Brian McGuire
Founder & Owner

I am the founder and Owner of M-Tac Trucking Compliance LLC.

I have recently retired after 25 years as a NJ State Trooper, and have spent the past 10 years in the NJ State Police / Transportation Safety Bureau enforcing the Federal Regulations governing USDOT Commercial Vehicles (3500+ inspections). 

In the last 6 years, I was assigned to the New Entrant Safety Audit / Compliance Review Unit, where I conducted over 1000 Safety Audits to ensure new carriers are educated and in compliance with FMCSA Regulations.
